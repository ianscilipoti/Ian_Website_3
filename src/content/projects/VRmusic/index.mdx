Telepresent Media is a small and nimble company working on projects built around telepresence, the concept of immersive experiences that allow for meaningful human connection at a distance.

Case Study: **Club Aloha**
===

Club Aloha is a Maui-based experimental music venue where concert-goers can share the thrill and intimacy of live music from anywhere in the world through virtual reality.
